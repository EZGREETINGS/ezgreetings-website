Frequently Asked Questions

When will EZ Greetings launch?

We're launching before Early Spring 2026. Sign up for updates by emailing support@ezgreetings.net.

How does EZ Greetings work?

You schedule personalized greetings for birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays. We automatically send them via SMS or email on the dates you specify. Learn more on our About page.

What are your privacy practices?

We take privacy seriously and never share your data with third parties for marketing. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Do you comply with TCPA regulations?

Yes, we fully comply with TCPA, CAN-SPAM, and all telecommunications regulations. See our Terms of Service for compliance details.

How can I report abuse?

If you receive unwanted messages from our service, please email abuse@ezgreetings.net immediately and we will investigate.
