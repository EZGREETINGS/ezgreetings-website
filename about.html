About EZ Greetings

Making it easy to stay connected with the people who matter most

What We Do

EZ Greetings is an automated greeting reminder service that helps you send personalized greetings to friends, family, and colleagues on important dates like birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays.

We believe that staying connected shouldn't require constant reminders and last-minute scrambles. With EZ Greetings, you set up your greetings once, and we handle the rest—delivering your personalized messages exactly when they're meant to be received.

How It Works

  1. Schedule Your Greetings - Add important dates and create personalized messages for the people you care about.
  2. Choose Delivery Method - Select SMS or email delivery based on your preference.
  3. Relax - We automatically send your greetings on the scheduled dates, year after year.

It's that simple. No apps to remember, no notifications to manage—just thoughtful greetings delivered on time, every time.

Why It Matters

In our busy lives, it's easy to let important dates slip by. A forgotten birthday or missed anniversary can feel like a small thing, but these moments matter. They're opportunities to show the people in our lives that we're thinking of them.

EZ Greetings exists to help you maintain those connections effortlessly, ensuring that the people who matter most to you always know they're remembered.

Our Commitment

Privacy First: Your messages and contact information are private. We never share your data with third parties, and we only use it to deliver the greetings you've scheduled.

Compliance: We follow all telecommunications regulations including TCPA and CAN-SPAM. Recipients can opt out at any time, and we honor all opt-out requests immediately.

Reliability: We use industry-leading infrastructure to ensure your greetings are delivered on time, every time.
